FAQ

What is HouseFilter?
HouseFilter is a web site that acts like your personal assistant in your search for your next house purchase. It keeps track of a list of houses you are considering, pulling in information about each house from multiple real-estate sites such as Zillow, Trulia, and Realtor.com, and helping you record your own notes about each house.
How is HouseFilter different from other real estate sites?
HouseFilter is based on the idea of easy and convenient sharing of information. By using HouseFilter, you are not limiting yourself to just one site. For example, using HouseFilter you can view information about a house from both Zillow and Realtor.com, and generate a list of houses you are interested in to email to your agent.
How much does HouseFilter cost to use?
You can use HouseFilter for free for 30 days. After that it costs just $10 per month. If you preorder, though, you can pay a single fee of $100 for a 1-year account, plus one share of our advertising revenue for the first year.
